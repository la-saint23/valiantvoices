Voice Discovery.

Through our trainers you are able to know your voice range. No pressure - this is just to help us support your musical journey!

Our Voice Parts

Soprano

Highest female voice

Typically sings melody lines and soaring harmonies. Range: C4-C6

Alto

Lower female voice

Provides rich harmonies and counter-melodies. Range: G3-G5

Tenor

Higher male voice

Often carries melody in male voices. Range: C3-C5

Bass

Lowest male voice

Provides foundation and rhythmic support. Range: E2-E4

Remember: Voice parts are flexible! Many singers can comfortably sing in multiple ranges, and we'll help you find where you feel most comfortable and confident.
